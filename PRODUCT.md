# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential clients, collaborators, recruiters, and technical partners visit this portfolio to understand Caleb Azumah's capabilities, judgment, project history, and availability. They are usually scanning quickly before deciding whether to explore case studies or make contact.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Caleb's full-stack engineering work, selected projects, experience, and contact path. Success means a visitor can quickly understand what Caleb builds, trust the quality of the work, and move naturally into a project detail, resume download, or contact conversation.

## Brand Personality

Confident, precise, capable. The tone should feel technical and polished without becoming cold, generic, or template-like.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page patterns, overstuffed card grids, fake decorative metrics, stock portfolio layouts, and repeated section scaffolding that makes every fold feel the same.

## Design Principles

Show engineering range through real examples and concrete details.
Let hierarchy do the work before adding decoration.
Use motion to guide attention, not to distract from the content.
Make contact and next steps obvious without turning the page into a sales funnel.
Keep the portfolio distinctive enough to feel authored, not assembled from a template.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard access. Respect reduced-motion preferences and keep body copy readable across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
